ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Kavan Dharia and I am a fourth-year Computer Science major at the University of California, Irvine (UCI).

I have a keen interest in Software Development. It fascinates me how different types of software can be combined together in order to create numerous high-functioning products that is used in our everyday lives.

Recently, I interned as an Aircraft and Simulator Systems Software Engineer for TRU Simulation + Training, under Textron Aviation, where I worked on Front-End Development to improve the usabilty of Instructor Operator Station (IOS) of Full Flight Simulators (FFS). Also, I worked for UCI INsite Lab as a Research Assistant where I helped blind community members learn how to use their smartphones during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Currently, I am looking for an entry level Software Engineering/Development role starting in Summer 2022. I am open to doing Front-End, Back-End, or Full-Stack Software Development.

In my free time, I enjoy playing sports such as Basketball, Spikeball, and Adult Men's Softball as well as being outdoors such as hiking, surfing, and snowboarding.
